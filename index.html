Guitar Techniques

Flamenco Rasgueado

Traditional Spanish technique for strumming where individual fingers fan out rapidly.

Picado

A technique for playing rapid single-note runs with alternating fingers.

Golpe

Percussive tapping on the soundboard of the guitar for rhythmic effect.
